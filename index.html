Digital Credentials And Identity for web 3.0

Streetcred provides a platform for managing decentralized identities and verifiable credentials. We give organization a unique way to become issuers of digital credentials and control a virtual agency to facilitate the communication with the decentralized ecosystem.
For users, we provide a way to manage their identities and store credentials in a secure wallet. Users have the benefit of selective disclosure to prove claims about them without revealing sensitive information.
Finally, our platform is built with developers in mind. We provide libraries and services that allows developers to build their own implementation or integrate their platforms with us.

Our Platform

Built for Sovrin

Our platform is built on top of the principles and standards set by the Sovrin Foundation.
Sovrin is a decentralized, global public utility for self-sovereign identity. Self-sovereign means a lifetime portable identity for any person, organization, or thing.

Complete Solution

Our Agent Portal provides organizations an easy to work with credentials. Easily review, issue and revoke credentials and relationships. Our mobile app gives users secure wallet for keeping and their credentials. Easy backup, provide proofs and communicate with the digital identity ecosystem.
